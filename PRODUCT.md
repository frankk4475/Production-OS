# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Production Managers & Coordinators**: Managing tasks, shot lists, crew scheduling, and documents via desktop computers. Need rapid data visualization and easy controls.
- **Crew & Directors**: Reviewing shooting schedules, master calendars, and tasks on-set, often using mobile devices in variable lighting. Need high-contrast displays and quick access.

## Product Purpose
Production OS is an integrated project management and scheduling platform designed specifically for film, commercial, and video productions. It streamlines script breakdown, story planning, crew scheduling, and document hub management into a single, cohesive, production-ready system.

## Brand Personality
- **Sleek**: Obsidian-based backgrounds and clean borders.
- **Premium**: Muted gold accents and elegant typography (Outfit/Inter).
- **Cinematic**: A dark-mode first, professional tools aesthetic that mirrors professional editing/color-grading suites.

## Anti-references
- Plain white SaaS spreadsheets that lack character.
- Over-saturated, bright corporate dashboards with generic icons and neon colors.
- Complex menus with high cognitive load.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Under Any Light**: Ensure visual readability with proper contrast, especially in dark mode for on-set mobile usage.
- **Context is King**: Keep navigation simple and show project-specific information cleanly without clutter.
- **Functional Animation**: Use subtle micro-interactions to indicate state transitions and loading states without causing distraction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast values for text elements (WCAG AA standard).
- Clear focus and active states.
- Clean typography supporting both Thai and English.
